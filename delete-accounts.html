Account Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your account from our app, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to accounts@sollibyte.com.
  2. Include your registered email address and password in the email.

Please note that the account deletion process will take up to 72 hours to complete. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been successfully deleted.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team.
